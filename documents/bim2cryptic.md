## bim2cryptic
**description**
*  a command to identify cryptic relatedness in plink binaries. individuals who are identified as having excessive relatedness are reporter in ```bim2cryptic.remove```

**syntax**

```bim2cryptic, bim(-filename-)```
 
*  ```-filename-``` does not require the .bim filetype to be included - this is assumed

**installation**

```net install bim2cryptic,         from(https://raw.github.com/ricanney/stata/master/code/b/) replace```
